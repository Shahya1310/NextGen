Bio - Degradable

Biodegradable wastes can be broken down by natural factors like microbes, temperature, UV, and oxygen. Examples include food materials and kitchen waste.

Learn More

Non - Biodegradable

Non-degradable substances cannot be broken down by natural organisms and remain in the environment for thousands of years, causing pollution and harming human life and wildlife.
