Harvest

In the early morning, select and harvest the stems of the lotus plant when they are fresh and full of sap.

Extract

The stems are cut to reveal the fine, sticky fibers inside, which are then pulled out by hand.

Spinning

Twist the fibers together to form a continuous thread.

Wash and Dye

Wash the fabric and dye it using natural dyes.
